Core journey

Healthcheck. Briefing. Review.

That is the client journey. Everything starts with a Healthcheck. The Briefing validates the picture and identifies what matters. A Review goes deeper where the organisation needs a more detailed evaluation.

01
Start here

Healthcheck

The website-based starting point. It establishes an initial baseline and gives KHITServices the context needed for a meaningful consultant-led conversation.

02
Consultant-led

Briefing

A focused discussion that validates the Healthcheck, explores organisational context and produces a clear view of what needs attention.

03
Deeper evaluation

Review

A more detailed evaluation where the Briefing shows that greater depth is needed. The Review provides structured findings and recommendations.

Ongoing support

Two ongoing models. Nothing else.

After the core journey, ongoing support is deliberately simple. Service Advisor is for small teams. VSDM is for growing businesses.

For small teams

Service Advisor

Lightweight ongoing advice for small teams that need KHITServices alongside them without creating unnecessary complexity.

  • Proportionate guidance.
  • Ongoing independent perspective.
  • Support without replacing existing providers.
For growing businesses

Virtual SDM

Ongoing Service Management for growing businesses that need structured governance, supplier oversight and continuing service assurance.

  • Independent Service Management oversight.
  • Governance and assurance support.
  • Ongoing improvement focus.
